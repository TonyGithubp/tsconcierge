TS Concierge offers a range of services tailored to meet your gardening and lawn care needs. Whether it's lawn mowing, planting, weeding, hedge trimming, or cleanup, we handle each task with utmost care and an unwavering commitment to detail.


Gardening

Unruly gardens full of overgrown weeds can be very annoying. Regular care maintenance is needed to keep your garden thriving, bringing you the natural beauty and joy.

We provide the following gardening services:


Weeding. Overgrown weeds can be very bushy and compete with your plants for water, nutrients, and sunlight. We do hand-pulling weeds very thoroughly. We refrain from using chemical herbicides to safeguard the health of your family, pets, and the environment.

Trimming & Pruning helps maintain plant health, shape, and size by removing dead or diseased branches so as to promote new growth. We do both plants and hedges trimming.

Mulching helps retain soil moisture, suppress weeds, regulate soil temperature, and provides essential benefits for plant health and growth. We help you place and spread mulch in garden beds.

Planting. Elevate your garden with our planting techniques, whether you're starting from scratch or looking to refresh your existing landscape.


Lawn Mowing

Regular lawn mowing is essential for the health and appearance of your lawn. At TS Concierge, our lawn mowing service includes:


Lawn Mowing. For regular maintenance, we can create a customized schedule that works for you, whether you prefer weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly mowing services. We also specialise in mowing and whipper snipping long overgrown lawns.

Edging. In addition to mowing, we provide thorough edging to define the borders of your lawn and give it a polished look. We pay close attention to detail, carefully edging and trimming around areas such as flower beds, walkways, and driveways, etc.

Cleanup. We take pride in leaving your property immaculate after every mowing service. We will removes grass clippings, debris, and any stray trimmings, leaving your lawn looking pristine and well-groomed.
