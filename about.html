Welcome to TS Concierge – Garden & Lawn Care,

where we believe in making life easier for our clients, one garden at a time.

The journey began with a simple dream – to provide assistance to those who need it. As the founder, I understand the challenges of balancing daily chores with other commitments, particularly for those with little to no time, physical limitations, or those who simply prefer not to do it themselves. Indeed, I sought to create a business where I could contribute to society while making a living.

With over 15 years of experience in the construction industry, I bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to our services. From laborer to supervisor, I've worked in various roles, honing my skills in construction, surveying, and project management. But, having lived on a rural property for several years, it was my passion for maintaining my own acreage gardens and lawns that sparked the idea of turning this endeavor into a full-blown gardening service.

At TS Concierge, we offer a range of services tailored to meet your gardening and lawn care needs. From planting and weeding to lawn mowing and hedge trimming, we handle it all with care, precision, and attention to detail.

With TS Concierge, you can trust that your outdoor yards are in capable hands. Let us help you reclaim your time and enjoy a beautiful, well-maintained garden and lawn at an affordable price.

Thank you for considering TS Concierge. We are eager to assist you soon.
